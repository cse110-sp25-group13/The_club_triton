Game Guide

  • Each turn, both the student and professor choose a card to play.
  • The green living card beats the blue dining card
  • The blue dining card beats the gray structure card
  • The gray structure card beats the green living card
  • When the cards are of the same type, the higher rarity beats them

How to win

  • There are 2 paths to achieve victory in this game
  • The first path is to collect 3 types of the same card i.e. 3 blue dining cards
  • The second is to collect 3 unique card types i.e. at least 1 dining, 1 living, and 1 strcuture card
  • The first to either path wins, good luck young triton!
